Scrambled Eggs

Description:

A blend of garlic and cilantro with scrambled eggs. A refreshing way to start your day.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon sour cream
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons freshly grated Parmesan cheese
