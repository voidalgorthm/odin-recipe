Garlic Cilantro Scrambled Eggs

Description:

A blend of garlic and cilantro with scrambled eggs. A refreshing way to start your day.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon sour cream
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Steps:

  1. Whisk together eggs, sour cream, milk, and garlic until smooth
  2. Add cilantro and season to taste with salt and pepper.
  3. Melt butter in a nonstick skillet over medium heat.
  4. Pour in egg mixture and cook to desired degree of doneness, stirring constantly.
  5. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese to serve.
